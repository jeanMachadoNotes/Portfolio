About Me

Born to a family of legendary Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu fighters, Jean Machado took his discipline and training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu to the stage. Beginning with theater in school he then began building a following on Youtube through light-hearted sketch comedy videos about Martial Arts, relationships, growing up with foreign parents, etc., amassing over 90,000 subscribers and 38.6 millions views. See more on Youtube.


Getting technical

With a strong interest in the technical aspects of cinema, Jean took to learning development. Eight months later, graduating from The Tech Academy in Web Development in June 2022.
