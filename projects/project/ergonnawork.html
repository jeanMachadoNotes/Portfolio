What is Ergonomics?

"for every minute spent organizing, an hour is earned." - Benjamin Franklin

Origin:

The Oxford Dictionary defines Ergonomics as:

"noun :the study of working conditions, especially the design of equipment and furniture, in order to help people work more efficiently.

from Greek ergon ‘work’+ nemein 'to manage' "

What is it?:

So Ergonomics has to do with,   how to manage how one works.

The science of making your work enviroment more comfortable.
